Delight in our sushi rolls. bubble tea. bingsu. udon. poke bowls. bento.

At Roll N Tea, we serve a delightful fusion of Korean and Japanese cuisine.

From our delicately handcrafted sushi rolls to our delicious bulgogi bento, we are home to a beautiful palette of Japanese and Korean foods to satisfy your cravings. On top of that, we serve some of the best bubble tea in all of Richmond Hill.
